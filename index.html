My Projects

  • Smart Agriculture System

    An IoT based project using Arduino, Node MCU, BLYNK App to help the farmer by decreasing the human labor in recording data related to soil condition and solve the problems faced in agriculture. The system also consisted of an automated irrigation pump that can also be controlled using the mobile app depending on the soil conditions visible in the app itself.

  • Portable Biometric Attendance System

    An IoT based project using Arduino, Node MCU, Firebase for making the attendance taking process more time efficient and less tiring by creating a module that can be passed to the students without interrupting the teacher's flow while teaching and also saving the time used in taking attendance and preventing proxies.

  • Smart Classroom

    The project consists of automated switching and intensity of lights and fans, an indication of empty classrooms, a portable bio-metric attendance system, and a test app. The project aims at making the classrooms automated, thus saving time and increasing the efficiency of the class. It also saves power and reduces the time consumption in attendance taking process.
